The Beginning

A Vision of Excellence

LUXE was born from a simple yet profound belief: that exceptional craftsmanship and timeless design should be accessible to those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Founded in 1985 by master artisan Jean-Pierre Dubois in the heart of Paris, our maison began as a small atelier dedicated to creating bespoke leather goods for a discerning clientele.

Today, LUXE has evolved into a global symbol of luxury fashion, while remaining true to our founding principles of quality, authenticity, and understated elegance.

Jean-Pierre Dubois Founder & Creative Director
